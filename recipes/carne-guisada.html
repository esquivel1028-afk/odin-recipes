Carne Guisada

There are so many ways to make carne guisada. Families have passed similar recipes from generation to generation.

You should find ways to modify the recipe to meet you and/or your families tastes!

INGREDIENTS

  • 3-lb. Chuck roast.
  • 1 White Onion-sliced
  • Green Bell Peppers
  • 2 Garclic cloves-chopped
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Powdered Cumin
  • 4 c. beef broth

STEPS

  1. Set your slow cooker to 'Low'
  2. Season your meet with salt, pepper and cumin. Place in the slow cooker insert already inside the heating element.
  3. Placed sliced onion, green bell peppers and chopped garlic on top of the meat.
  4. Pour the beef broth over vegetables and meat
  5. Cover and cook on 'Low' for 6-8 hours or until meat falls apart
  6. Remove lid, turn off the slowcooker and let it sit for 5-10 minutes.
  7. Enjoy in tortillas or with a side of rice and beans
