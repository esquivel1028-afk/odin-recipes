Lasagna

This is a lasagna recipe that you and your family will enjoy!

It is such a simple recipe you can have your kids watch and learn a long with you!

INGREDIENTS

  • Lasagna noodles-oven ready
  • 28 oz jar spaghetti sauce
  • Ground beef-browned and drained
  • 8oz ricotta cheese
  • 16oz grated mozzarella cheese-divided
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley-optional

STEPS

  1. Preheat oven to 350 Degrees F
  2. Combine sauce and cooke ground beef into a warm-proof bowl
  3. Combine ricotta cheese and 8oz mozzarella cheese in a separate bowl
  4. Spoon and spread 1 cup of sauce and ground beef mixture on the bottom of an oven-safe dish
  5. Lay 3 lasagna noodle parallel to each other on top of the mixture
  6. Spoon 1 cup of sauce/beef mixture over the noodles
  7. Spread half of the cheese mixture on top of the sauce/beef mixture
  8. Repeat steps 5-7 one more time
  9. Lay 3 more lasagna noodles on top
  10. Spread the remaining sauce/beef mixture on top
  11. Spread the remaining mozzarella cheese on top
  12. Cover the dish with foil and place in the preheated oven for 45 minutes
  13. Using oven mits, remove the dish from the oven and let rest for 10 minute before enjoying!
